About Us

Mathematics is a challenging issue in SA, due to the perception embedded in a student's mind that mathematics is hard, and this leads tostudents having a negative attitude towards it. Math-Aid does not only assist students with improving their marks but it also aims to change the attitudethat students have towards mathematics. Math-Aid aims to provide students with a mind concept that makes maths exciting and achievable. A posite attitude will assist leaners to be more earger to learn and excel in maths.

Vision

Math-Aid aims to simplify maths and make it reletable to students.

Mission

Stimulate student's interest and apreciation of maths through effective teaching.
Provide training through the use of reliable mathematical books.
Equip student with problem solving skills via group interaction or one on one session.
Providing a learning environment that enables students to practice their maths skills.
Transforming the fearful attitude that learners have towards maths to a positive and exciting one.
Offering time and stress management to help students function better.
